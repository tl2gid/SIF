APPLICATION PERIOD

The first: September 16, 2022 to October 16, 2022
Secondary : Recruitment scheduled when additional booth quantity is secured

참가 신청 절차

1.신청

참가신청 일정에 맞취 참가신청서 접수
접수 시 포트폴리오 함께 제출

2.검수

신청서 접수일로부터 4주~6주 간 사무국에서 검수 진행
검수 완료 후 참가신청 결과 개별 이메일로 안내

3.결과

참가 확정인 경우, 참가확정 및 입금안내 메일 발송
미확정 시 대기 접수 개별 이메일로 안내

4.확정

참가 확정 메일 수신 일주일 내 계약금 입금
기간 내 미입금시 참가 확정 자동 취소

APPLICATION PRODEDURES

1. Sign up

Reception of the application for participation in accordance with the participation application schedule, and submission of the portfolio together upon receipt

2. Examine

The office will conduct an inspection for four to six weeks from the date of application receipt, After the inspection is completed, the results of the participation application are notified by individual email

3. Result

If the participation is confirmed, send an email confirming the participation and deposit information, If it is not confirmed, wait reception will be notified by individual email

4. Be Decided

Deposit the down payment within a week of receiving the confirmation mail, Automatic cancellation of participation confirmation when payment is not made within the period
